Workout Routine

Chest Workout

  • Pushups

    Effect: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and triceps, improves core stability.

  • Incline Fly

    Effect: Targets upper chest, improves muscle definition.

  • Flat Dumbbell Press

    Effect: Enhances chest strength, builds size and shape.

  • Decline Rod Press

    Effect: Focuses on lower chest, increases muscle mass.

  • Lower Cross-over

    Effect: Engages lower chest and adds muscle tone.

Cardio Routine

  • Mountain Climbing

    Effect: Full-body workout, enhances endurance and agility.

  • Rope CrossFit

    Effect: Burns calories, boosts stamina and coordination.

  • Lying Cycling

    Effect: Strengthens core, improves leg muscle endurance.

  • Treadmill

    Effect: Burns fat, increases cardiovascular health.

  • Cycling

    Effect: Boosts cardiovascular health, tones lower body.
